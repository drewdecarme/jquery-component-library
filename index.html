Welcome!

Over the past couple of years, I’ve been experimenting with different development techniques in order to make my life a little bit easier when developing front ends for myself, my clients, or my current organization. I've finally been able to take the time to package up some of my work to put on display for the web-o-sphere to critique, so here we are.

Here you’ll find a collection of some of the common components that you’ll often see and interact with in most web applications. The only caveot is that I've attempted to take them to the next level and provide an additional layer of easy to use configurations. In addition to the examples, you’ll also find some in-depth descriptions on my thought process, development style, and rationale for creation. I created these to not only make my life easier, but to also experiment and learn some of the more abstract concepts of JavaScript.

Feel free to mosey around and take a look at my work. I'll be periodically updating this with more use cases and widgets as time goes on. If you love it, I’d love the feedback, and if you hate it, I’d still love the feedback so feel free to reach out to me with your thoughts!

Enjoy!
